What is Youooo Academy?

Youooo Academy is the main learning hub for the Youooo ecosystem. It brings together skill-building tracks, portfolio-style projects, and career-focused tools in one place.

Is Youooo Resume part of the academy?

Yes. Youooo Resume is one of the main family products and should be featured as a core part of the Academy experience, not hidden as a side link.

Who is this for?

It is designed for learners, career changers, builders, and anyone who wants to move from reading content to creating real public-facing work.

What should come next after V2?

The next upgrade should add real links for every family product, stronger track pages, and a more complete CTA flow so the homepage turns visitors into active users.
